Keith Hayden: The Digital Novelist

About Me

Hi, I'm Keith Hayden, a digital novelist with a passion for telling original stories with technology. My journey as a writer started in 2004. That was the year I started journaling after my dad passed away from an unexpected stroke. Since then, I have been creating immersive worlds that blend technology with storytelling. I believe in pushing the boundaries of traditional writing to create experiences that captivate readers in a age of acceleration.

Milestones

  • 2004: Began journaling.
  • 2017: Published first (non-fiction) book The Tower of Babbling.
  • 2021: Published debut novel, military game tech fiction story Cereus & Limnic.
  • May 2024: Began work on second novel. A supernatural horror story called Gates of Okinawa

Quotes from My Work

"The candidate who was the best reflection of that society, in that moment, would lead the institution For better or worse." – From *Cereus & Limnic: Second Evolution*
"Everything felt too big or too small to ask God for. It wasn’t right." – From *Gates of Okinawa*
